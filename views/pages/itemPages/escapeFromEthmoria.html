Escape from Ethmoria

Embark on a harrowing odyssey through the desolate lands of Ethmoria in "Escape from Ethmoria", a bone-chilling roleplaying game adventure that delves into the eerie aftermath of a fallen kingdom. Once ruled by a malevolent Lich Queen whose dark reign spanned centuries, Ethmoria now lies shattered and forsaken, haunted by the echoes of its sinister past. As brave adventurers, you must navigate treacherous landscapes and unravel the mysteries shrouding this forsaken realm, confronting vengeful spirits, eldritch horrors, and the lingering specter of the Lich Queen herself. Prepare to test the limits of courage and resilience as you chart a perilous course towards redemption or damnation in the haunted ruins of Ethmoria.
